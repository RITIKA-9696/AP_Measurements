Benefits of Owning a AP Measurement Shock Dyno

Discover how a shock dyno can transform your workshop and improve your suspension tuning capabilities.

Diagnose Faulty Shock Absorbers or Forks

A shock dyno can quickly identify and diagnose common issues with shock absorbers or forks within seconds. By examining the force vs displacement graph, you can easily understand what is happening with the damper during compression and rebound. Problems such as blocked oil passages, broken shims, cavitation caused by low gas pressure, and more can all be rapidly detected through detailed dyno graph analysis.

Compare Performance

Effortlessly visualize and analyze changes made to your shock absorber or fork with advanced comparison features.

Comparison Applications:

  • Left vs right dampers
  • Front vs back dampers
  • Different damper settings
  • Before-and-after tuning effects

Navigate Tuning and Adjustment

Make data-driven, precise adjustments by analyzing graph curves alongside rider feedback for fine-tuned suspension performance.

Tuning Advantages:

  • Clear visual feedback on adjustments
  • Correlation between adjustments and performance
  • Improved track or road performance
