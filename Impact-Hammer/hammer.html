Description

The NO2B00 General Purpose IEPE Impact Hammer is designed for modal testing and structural analysis applications. With its integrated electronics piezoelectric (IEPE) technology, this hammer eliminates the need for external charge amplifiers, providing a simplified and reliable solution for impact testing.

Featuring a sensitivity of 11.1mV/lbf and a range of 450 lbf pk, this impact hammer is suitable for a wide range of applications from small components to medium-sized structures. The built-in electronics ensure low-noise performance and excellent signal integrity.

Key Features

  • Integrated IEPE electronics - no external charge amp required
  • High sensitivity for accurate low-level force measurements
  • Wide temperature range for various environmental conditions
  • BNC connector for easy integration with data acquisition systems
  • Rugged construction for reliable performance in industrial settings
