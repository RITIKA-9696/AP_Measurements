Technology Comparison EMA vs Hydraulic Systems

Understanding the fundamental differences between electromagnetic and hydraulic damper testing technologies.

Electromagnetic Actuators

Provide exceptional precision in force control with rapid response times. Excel across wide frequency ranges with quiet operation and minimal mechanical complexity. Require less maintenance with significantly reduced operational downtime.

Hydraulic Systems

Designed for higher force requirements in industrial applications, particularly at lower frequencies. Require regular maintenance due to fluid dynamics, potential leaks, and mechanical wear. Operate under more stringent safety requirements.
