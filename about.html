About LABA7

Pioneering precision in suspension testing technology since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a team of racing engineers and suspension specialists, LABA7 was born out of frustration with the limitations of existing suspension testing equipment. We saw a need for more accurate, reliable, and user-friendly shock dynos that could provide meaningful data to help teams and workshops optimize performance.

What started as a small workshop project has grown into an industry-leading company serving professional racing teams, OEM manufacturers, and performance workshops worldwide. Our commitment to innovation and quality has made LABA7 the preferred choice for those who demand precision in suspension testing.

Our Mission

To provide the most accurate, reliable, and user-friendly suspension testing equipment that empowers our customers to achieve peak performance through data-driven insights.
